Welcome to Simple 2 Do!

Simple 2 Do is easy to use!

Retreiving your todolists is as easy as typing it into the URL using this Structure:
{{$ctrl.prodUrl}}username/todolist

To get started, simply create a username by typing it below, or by typing it directly into the URL above like so:

{{$ctrl.prodUrl}}


Simple 2 Do has NO security. Your todolists can be easily shared with friends and family by sharing your username. If you'd like to keep a list private, think of your username more like a password.
