Welcome to Simple 2 Do!

Simple 2 Do is easy to use!

To get started, simply create a username by typing it below, or by typing it directly into the URL above like so:

www.s2do.me/YourUserName


Simple 2 Do has NO security. Any todolist can be easily shared with friends and family by sharing your username. If you'd like to keep a list private, think of your username more as a password
